About Me

Hey there! I’m Indira Van Kanegan, a recent graduate from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill with a degree in Statistics and Analytics, and minors in Data Science and Computer Science. During my time at UNC, I developed a deep passion for both data analytics and web design. To me, these fields are like intricate brain games—both start with complex, open-ended challenges and involve creating something practical and user-focused.

In data science, the challenge is to sift through vast amounts of data to find patterns and derive actionable insights. On the flip side, web design begins with a blank canvas where creativity meets functionality to build an engaging, user-friendly experience. Both fields demand attention to detail and a mix of critical and creative thinking. Every project feels like solving a puzzle, where each choice—whether it’s a design element or a data algorithm—affects the final outcome.

Outside of my professional interests, I love cooking and baking - I’m currently working on mastering sourdough :) I also enjoy hiking, film and digital photography, and spending quality time with friends and family.
